Hire Info

This is how it works.

Booking

Call or email us anytime to enquire re availability, and once we have discussed your individual requirements, all we need is a 50% deposit to secure your BubbleTubs booking. The balance is then payable on delivery.


Delivery & Setup

We will deliver, install & set up all equipment…all we need is access to an electrical point. We will provide a weatherproof box, which has a 10 meter lead. This can either run from an outdoor socket, or through a window/door to an indoor socket (windows & doors can still be locked). The 5 plug adaptor will then run the hot tub & any electrical extras we have supplied.


Access

We will also need access to your water supply (indoor or outdoor) so we can fill your tub…& whilst your tub is filling, we’ll assemble the gazebo. All-in-all, installation & set-up should only take between 40 minutes to an hour, depending on your water pressure.


Demo & Instructions

Once everything is up & running, we’ll go through some very simple operating & maintenance guidelines…Then we can leave you to enjoy your fabulous hot tub experience!


Collection

On collection, we’ll simply require access to a drain or sink to empty the water from the hot tub.

FAQ

Please browse our list of frequently asked questions below, if you dontfind an answer youo need please give us a call or contact us using the form below.

1How much electricity will it use?

Approximately 7-10 pounds a week. The Layzspa pump heater is thermostatically regulated, allowing it to intelligently heat the hot tub to save on running costs, much like the central heating system; heating the water to your desired temperature then switching off. It will then switch back on again when the temperature falls a few degrees below this. The hot tub also comes with a top cover & inflatable disc to provide maximum insulation.

2How long will it take to heat up?

Approximately 10 hours from cold to maximum temperature. This can be accelerated by filling with hot water but may increase the fill time if the flow rate of hot water is less than that of the cold supply.

3What is the maximum temperature the hot tub can reach?

40 degrees…You wouldn’t want it any hotter than this!

4Are inflatable hot tubs comfortable?

Yes, very! The base is inflated & padded, which makes them extremely comfortable to relax in.

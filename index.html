About Us

Quality hot tub hire at surprisingly low prices. We pride ourselves on customer satisfaction.

BubbleTubs is a family-run inflatable hot tub hire business, based in Holmfirth, West Yorkshire. We hire hot tubs for all occasions…Birthdays…Anniversaries…Hen parties…Kids’ parties…Romantic evenings…Garden parties…End of exam celebrations…Halloween…Christmas/New Years Eve…or even just for a little bit of ‘You’ time, to relax and unwind after a hectic week!
